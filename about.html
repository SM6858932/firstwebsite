About GlobalPulse

Your intelligent window to the world — curating breaking news, trending stories, and meaningful content from trusted global sources.

"Empowering readers with a smarter, faster way to stay informed. We believe quality journalism should be accessible, engaging, and rewarding."

— The GlobalPulse Team

Our Story

GlobalPulse was born from a simple observation: the world is generating more news than ever, but most people don't have time to navigate dozens of different websites to stay informed. Traditional news aggregators either overwhelm you with clutter or provide shallow, context-free headlines.

We set out to build something different — a platform that not only brings you the latest headlines from trusted sources around the world but also adds value through curated summaries, context, and engaging features that make staying informed feel rewarding.

Our Sources

We source headlines and summaries from publicly available RSS feeds provided by:

  • International: BBC News, The New York Times, TechCrunch, The Verge, CNBC
  • India: NDTV, Times of India, The Hindu, Economic Times, Mint, Gadgets360, Indian Express, Cricbuzz

We always provide clear attribution and links back to original sources. We encourage readers to visit the original articles for full coverage.

Original Analysis

Beyond aggregation, we publish original analysis articles that provide deeper insights into the stories shaping our world. These include explainers, trend analysis, and thought leadership pieces that go beyond the headlines. See our Articles section for original content.

Our Values

  • Accuracy: We source from reputable publications and clearly attribute all content.
  • Transparency: We clearly distinguish between curated news and original analysis.
  • Privacy: We respect your data and are transparent about how we use it. See our Privacy Policy.
  • Engagement: We believe learning about the world should be an active, rewarding experience.

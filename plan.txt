The Content Plan: Auto-Fetching Viral News

For a news site that changes content daily or weekly, you need a fully automated setup:


Tool: Install WP RSS Aggregator Pro (if on WordPress) or build a custom feed fetcher using Python/RSS.

Setup: Feed it RSS URLs of top national news portals (ensure you respect copyright/fair use by summarizing, not copying full articles).

AI Rewriting (Optional but Recommended): Use the  Auto Robot plugin to automatically rewrite the fetched headlines and create a unique 2-line summary. This keeps Google from penalizing you for duplicate content.

Owner-Posted Reels & Products (Not Users)

Since you (the website owner) are the sole content creator for reels and products:

For Reels (Short Videos): Create a custom "Video" post type. Use a video hosting service like Vimeo or Bunny.net (to save bandwidth costs) and embed them. Alternatively, just embed your own YouTube/Instagram Shorts directly onto the page. Since you post them, you control the narrative—post daily trending viral reels to keep users refreshing the page.

For Products: Set up WooCommerce. List your own merchandise, digital downloads (e-books, courses), or dropshipping products. Since you aren't relying on users to list items, you have full quality control.

Display Strategy: Place these Reels and Products in the sidebar or between news paragraphs so they get maximum exposure without annoying the user.

Building Followers & Engagement (Even if Users Don't Post)

Even if users don't upload content, they can still engage. Here is how to get followers without user-generated content:

Push Notifications: Integrate OneSignal (free up to 30K subscribers). Add a "Subscribe to Breaking News" button. Every time you update the page with new viral news, send a push notification. This builds a loyal returning audience.

Comment Section: Install Disqus or wpDiscuz and allow users to comment, like, and reply to your posts.

Social Login: Use "Login with Google/Facebook" so users can easily create accounts to "save" or "bookmark" your posts and reels.

Gamification: Add a simple points system (using myCRED plugin) where users earn points for commenting, sharing on social media, or visiting daily. This drastically increases time-on-site.

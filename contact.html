Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the user avatar icon in the top-right corner of the homepage and select "Sign In / Sign Up." You can register with your name, email, and password.

How do points and streaks work?

You earn points by reading articles (+5), liking (+2), commenting (+15), sharing (+20), and daily visits (+10). Streaks track consecutive daily visits with bonus points. See the sidebar widget on the homepage for details.

Where does the news come from?

We aggregate headlines and summaries from publicly available RSS feeds of trusted sources including BBC, NY Times, TechCrunch, CNBC, The Verge, and leading Indian publications. We always link back to the original source.

How do I report a bug or issue?

You can open an issue on our GitHub repository or use the contact form above.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@globalpulse.news with your account email and we'll process the deletion request within 7 days.

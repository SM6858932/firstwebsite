# AdSense Plan

## Recommended starting point
- Enable Auto Ads first using the publisher ID.
- Keep the site content rich, original, and stable before adding more ad units.
- Make sure the site has clear navigation, useful content, and a consistent mobile layout.

## Best ad types for early approval
- Auto Ads: best first step for new sites.
- In-feed ads: good once the site has strong content sections.
- In-article ads: useful for long-form or article-heavy pages.
- Anchor ads: can be tested later, but they are usually better after the site is established.

## For future AFS / advanced ad setup
- Add a few manual ad units only after Auto Ads is approved and traffic is steady.
- Prefer placements that feel natural inside the content flow, such as between article cards and within the sidebar.
- Avoid overloading the page with too many ads at the start.
